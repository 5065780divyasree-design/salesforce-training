# What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It is a software system that helps companies manage their customers, sales, communication, and business activities in one place.

It helps businesses improve relationship and increase sales efficiency

CRM basically helps businesses work smarter and stay organized.

---

# Why Companies Use Salesforce

Salesforce is one of the most popular cloud-based CRM platforms in the world.

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:
- Customer data management
- Sales tracking
- automation tools
- Improve customer service
- report and dasboards
- Use AI-powered tools for smarter decisions

# Core Salesforce Concepts

## Account
An **Account** represents a company or organization.

### Example
- Amazon
- Infosys
- TCS

Accounts store business-related information like:
- Company name
- Industry
- Phone number
- Address

---

## Contact
A **Contact** represents a person connected to an Account.

Contacts usually contain:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Job role

---

## Opportunity
An **Opportunity** represents a potential sales deal.

### Example
If a company wants to purchase Salesforce services.
